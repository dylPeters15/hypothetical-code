• This application SKUs (Stock-Keeping Units) for the food items produced by the company. These details are provided by the administrator.
  • This application tracks the ingredients that are used to manufacture each SKU.
  • Manufacturing staff can use this application to calculate ingredient needs based on manufacturing goals.
  • Product managers can use this application to produce reports showing information about ingredients and the SKUs they are used to make.
  • Users are able to bulk-import and bulk-export ingredients and SKU information from simple text format.
